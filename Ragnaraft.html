Ragnaraft

Mobile Cooperative Academy Project

Role: Game Designer

About the Project

Bring your phone to the tavern and have an unforgettable night by cooperating with a friend in this ironic 2D top-down game.

Venture through different seasons as you float down the river, avoiding obstacles and hazards to reach the beautiful damsel.

This mobile project had one main objective: Create a cooperative experience playable on a single phone. Players control the game by simply pressing their fingers on opposite sides of the screen, encouraging local co-op play with intuitive controls.

During this project, I was mainly responsible for writing the Game Design Document (GDD). The project lasted almost two months, during which I significantly improved my skills in both game design and the technical side of Unity.
