The Bubble

Global Game Jam 2024 - Survival Horror

Role: Game Designer / Environment Detailing

About the Project

A pulsating green light echoes in this structure, you are not alone.

The Bubble is a survival horror game where you take part in the escape of the last member of a space crew from a mysterious entity.

The goal of the project was to create a gameplay experience similar to Alien: Isolation. The player must escape from a space station that has been overrun by a hostile alien creature. To progress, the player needs to find fuses and activate generators in order to unlock doors. Ultimately, the objective is to reach and board the rescue ship to escape.

This project was developed during Global Game Jam 2024, focusing on creating tension and atmospheric horror within tight time constraints.
